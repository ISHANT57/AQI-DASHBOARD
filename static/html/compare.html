Understanding AQI Comparison

When comparing AQI values between countries, consider these important factors:

  • Methodology: Different regions may use slightly different methods to calculate AQI values
  • Monitoring Coverage: Some countries have more comprehensive monitoring networks than others
  • Seasonal Variations: Air quality often varies significantly by season in many regions
  • Regional Factors: Geography, climate, and urbanization greatly impact air quality measurements
  • Time Periods: Ensure you're comparing data from the same time periods for accurate insights

Our comparison tools allow you to control for these variables to ensure you're making valid comparisons across different countries and regions.
